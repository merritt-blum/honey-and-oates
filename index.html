About Us

Honey & Oates is a musical duo who play an eclectic mix of acoustic covers and originals. We started our journey in 2021 and have been rocking ever since.

Kristina

Meet Kristina, the guitar-strumming, songbird half of Honey & Oates! With a voice as sweet as honey and guitar skills that get everyone grooving, she's the heart and soul of the band. Her infectious energy and spontaneous spirit turn every day into a fun adventure. Get ready to sing along and dance with Kristina—life's a jam session with her around!

Stephen

Meet Stephen, the other guitar-slinging, melody-making dynamo of Honey & Oates! With vocals that harmonize perfectly and guitar riffs that electrify, Stephen brings a cool energy to every performance. He can be found crafting new tunes or digging into the oldies. Join Stephen for an unforgettable musical journey—because with him, every day is a concert!
